Special features

Virus and threat protection

Monitor threats to your device, perform scans, and collect updates to support in spotting the most recent attacks. (Some of these choices are inaccessible if Windows 10 is operating in S mode.)

User security

Manage account settings and sign-in options, such as Windows Hello and dynamic lock.

Firewall and network security

Configure firewall settings and constantly check on your networks and internet connections.

Apps and browser control

Protect your devices against potentially harmful programs, files, websites, and downloads. You'll get exploit protection and the ability to adjust your device's protection settings.

Quarantine

The technique of isolating a virus-infected file in order to prevent it from infecting other sections of your computer.
